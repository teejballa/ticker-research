Agreement computation (RESEARCH.md lines 446-450 ‚Äî exact rules):
- `'aligned'` if (diffusion_posterior > 0.55 AND technical_posterior > 0.55) OR (diffusion < 0.45 AND technical < 0.45) ‚Äî AND both statuses are ACTIVE
- `'opposed'` if (diffusion > 0.6 AND technical < 0.4) OR (diffusion < 0.4 AND technical > 0.6) ‚Äî AND both statuses are ACTIVE
- `'mixed'` if both statuses are ACTIVE but neither aligned nor opposed
- `'unknown'` if either status is NO_DATA, EXPLORATORY, or DEPRECATED
